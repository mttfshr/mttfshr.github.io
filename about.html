iforwe Here's the new one. Iforwe is a hybrid exhibition project I'm developing and curating for Pro Arts Gallery in Oakland in 2018. The exhibition is about Bay Area artist collectives and cooperatives. It's an unusual exhibition; it will use its own open source app to gather, conceptualize and visualize connections between artist groups. The iforwe app is being developed using Vue.js framework and connects to the Facebook social graph. Aside from being my first large scale curatorial survey exhibition as curator, it's my first completely-from-scratch full stack app project. Follow the progress and get involved on Github.

San Francisco Bay Guardian archive An ongoing collaboration with the San Francisco Progressive Media Center (the owners of 48hills.org) and the San Francisco Center for Newspaper Preservation (the new owners of the Bay Guardian). We're gathering all 50 years worth of the alternative weekly's issues and blogs — spanning Drupal, HTML, PDF, electronic editorial, and pre-electric files — and converting them into a future-ready, responsive, searchable online archive. (Phase 1 just launched.) I'm database admin and UX/front end developer on this project.
